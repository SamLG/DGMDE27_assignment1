Winter 2016-2017.

This Winter Shows Promise

 This year so far is already better than last year. We have had at least one good week of below freezing, getting down into the single digits and below freezing. The cold temperatures should help cull the mosquitoes and ticks for the other seasons, especially since the snow fall has been so disappointing. At least without the layer of insulation between the ground and the cold air, we are even more likely to have decreased the parasite population.

 Just this past week (2/5/17) there has been significant snowfall and now us snow lovers get to bask in a bright winter wonderland. My dog Geordie has been thrilled and gets to bounce through a thick blanket of snow. Now we just get to hope that the temperature doesn't heat up and melt away our snow. We plan on going sledding this Saturday (2/18/17). There's just something about all that blinding white and heightened vitamin d that puts you in a good mood.
