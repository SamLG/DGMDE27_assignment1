This Winter Shows Promise. Winter 2016-2017.

 This year so far is already better than last year. We have had at least one good week of below freezing, getting down into the single digits and below freezing. The cold temperatures should help cull the mosquitoes and ticks for the other seasons, especially since the snow fall has been so disappointing. At least without the layer of insulation between the ground and the cold air, we are even more likely to have decreased the parasite population.
